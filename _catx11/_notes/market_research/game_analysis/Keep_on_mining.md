


# KEEP ON MINING:

IMO, this is the BEST EXAMPLE of a game that we should seek to model.

It just looks very very simple, and the game itself is clean and cool.


## Lessons to take from this game:
- Skill-tree is EXTREMELY simple (is just a grid)
    - (Skill tree nodes can be upgraded multiple times)

- Rewards are given automatically after level-up. Quite nice

- The idling is **VERY SIMPLE, AND VERY SATISFYING.**
- *Theming is internally consistent.*

## NEGATIVE-STEAM-REVIEW 1:
I wish I could recommend, but the game peters out before it every really takes off.

Pros:
- Relatively novel idea having "rounds" where you mine a lot then come back and upgrade.
- Upgrading your main tool was neat

Cons:
- Never opens up into something bigger--like I wanted to be mining asteroids or something by the end
- No really progressive OP upgrade, like constantly upgrading +X.
- No good stat tracking to see if you are getting more effective in your runs
- Idle mine seems half-baked, unnecessary to use


## NEGATIVE-STEAM-REVIEW 2:
Not much depth, game is fun the first 30 minutes. 
Then you just wait for upgrades. 
The "passive" mine feature is useless and brings nothing to a so called Idle game. 
Not worth it's money compared to other idlers.



## NEGATIVE-STEAM-REVIEW 4:
I liked playing the game a lot. It is very satisfying and has good graphics and sounds but it is way to short.
The game is 5 bucks, my rule of thump is a game should give me 1 hour of gameplay for every buck spent.
I managed to beat the game and get all achievement in less than 3,5 hours.
Some talents are completly worthless and even the artifacts doesn't seem powerful enough to make a real difference.
But even with this major flaws i admit it was fun and has a lot of potential for improvements like:
- let me get Artifacts multiple times to make them stronger
- a prestige system like other incrementals
- Other or bigger maps so improving mining area and other upgrades feel more important and also give the ability to improve them even further without breaking the game immediatly
- If the game gets longer and bigger an exchange mechanic for your bars would be great
- Improve the Crafting system. Why only craft a pickaxe? let me Craft improvements for my mine or tools to get more Upgrades!

However it was nice but felt more like a demo than a real game and i wouldn't recommend to buy the game at the current state, but I realy hope that there is more to come.
