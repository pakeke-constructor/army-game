## LMM (ChatGPT) review summary:

# 🔑 Combined Idle Game Review Insights:
## Core Loop & Longevity
- Players enjoy simple, satisfying feedback (visuals/sounds, number go up).
- Games lose steam fast if runs feel the same — meta-progression must make resets feel faster or different.
- Infinite content isn’t realistic, but late-game needs at least some meaningful milestones or twists to keep players motivated.

## Progression & Systems
- Prestige upgrades must feel impactful (not tiny or overly specific).
- True idle features matter — automation, offline gains, and reduced micromanagement are expected.
- Big upgrades that create a “power fantasy” (exponential growth, breaking past limits) are highly valued.

## Experience & Presentation
- Cohesive theming and clear UI elevate even simple mechanics (Keep-on-Mining).
- QoL is critical: autobuy, stat tracking, saving settings — players resent busywork.
- Performance/stability issues quickly sour impressions.

## Formula for success:
- Early game → satisfying, flashy, simple.
- Mid-game → varied systems, impactful meta, automation/QoL.
- Late game → Meaningful milestones or new twists

## LLM (Claude) summary:

### Core Progression Mechanics
Multiple currencies and resources that unlock over time (not just one number going up)
Prestige/rebirth systems that reset progress but provide permanent bonuses
Automation features that gradually reduce the need for active clicking
Exponential scaling with meaningful breakpoints and milestones

### Engagement Systems
Offline progress so players benefit from time away
Short-term goals (next upgrade) balanced with long-term objectives (major unlocks)
Achievement systems that reward different play styles
Random events or mini-games to break up the core loop

### Progression Variety
Multiple upgrade paths and strategic choices
New mechanics that unlock at different stages (not just bigger numbers)
Visual progression - seeing your "empire" grow beyond just text updates
Time-gated content that gives players reasons to return regularly

### Quality of Life Features
Clear information about what upgrades do and their efficiency
Notation systems for large numbers that remain readable
Save/export functionality
Options for different play speeds or automation levels

### Retention Elements
Daily bonuses or login rewards. Seasonal events or limited-time content
Social features like leaderboards or sharing milestones
Regular content updates with new prestige layers or mechanics
