

# TOKENS:

"Tokens" are what spawns to be harvested.
Think of it like the "deck" that the player has.

When a player mines a token, after 0.5 seconds, the token respawns.

Different tokens have more/less health, and give different rewards.


---

TOKEN EXAMPLES:
- Grass: +$1
- Log: +$8, +1 logs
- Mushroom: +$10
- Rock: +$100, +1 rock
...
- Granite: +15 rocks
- Gold: +$1000, +10 rocks
- Slime: +$10000, +1 bone
- Skull: +2 bones

-==========================================  

To start with, players just have Grass and Logs.   
However, they improve their "deck" over time.




# Prestige-system:
If we allow player to keep "stacking up" tokens infinitely, it will turn sour.  
there will be too much shit on screen.   
I propose a prestige-system:

Once you have 20 grass-tokens, you can "prestige" into the next tier:  
`20 grass -> 3 mushrooms`   
(And as a result, you lose ALL of your grass)
- 20 grass = $20  
- But 3 mushrooms $30, so its worth it.

This is a very elegant and straightforward system that works really well, IMO.
