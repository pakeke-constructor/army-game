

## Event ideas:











### BOSS-SIGNAL EVENTS:
Every boss has guaranteed events you are guaranteed to stumble across.
event does nothing, but has themed text:

FROST BOSS:
The ground seems cold, and there is a frosty tension in the air.
Passing merchants tell tales of farmers being frozen solid, during the light of day...

BIG BOSS:
During the night, your soldiers are woken up by a tremendous thundering, far far away. Almost like footsteps... like a mountain in the distance was moving

SNAKE BOSS:
Your soldiers are having nightmares of snake-people. They complain that they are being threatened and taunted. Is it a coincidence? A case of mass-hysteria? Or is there a sinister curse brewing...

OTHER BOSS:
A villager who is travelling with you breaks out into a fit of madness:
"The eyes! I see the eyes! Eyes everywhere, we will be DOOMED!"
One of your soldiers knocks him out with a stick. Silly rumours, nothing to worry about.
